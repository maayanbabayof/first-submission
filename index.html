This project introduces an innovative digital platform designed to bridge the gap between farmers in need of manpower and volunteers seeking meaningful opportunities in the agricultural sector. The platform, leveraging cutting-edge technology aims to streamline the process of connecting farmers with suitable volunteers. volunteer availability, farmer requirements, and geographic locations, the system facilitates efficient and mutually beneficial matches.
